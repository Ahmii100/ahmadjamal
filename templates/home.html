Questions About My Expertise

android

Android Development

What Android versions do you support? +

I develop for Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and above, covering 95%+ of active devices. For enterprise apps, I can target specific versions as needed.

Do you build both native and cross-platform? +

I specialize in native Android (Kotlin/Java) for optimal performance. For cross-platform needs, I recommend and implement Flutter/React Native based on project requirements.

How do you handle app security? +

I implement industry best practices: data encryption, secure API communication, ProGuard/R8 obfuscation, and regular security audits to protect user data.

How long does app development take? +

Simple apps: 4-6 weeks, Medium complexity: 2-3 months, Complex enterprise apps: 4-6 months. I provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide post-launch support? +

Yes! I offer 3 months of free bug fixes and support. Extended maintenance packages are available for ongoing updates and improvements.

SEO Specialist

How soon can I see SEO results? +

You'll start seeing initial improvements in 20-25 days! Complete optimization takes 3-6 months for maximum results, but rankings begin improving within weeks.

What tools do you use for SEO? +

I use Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Analytics 4, Search Console, Screaming Frog, and custom analytics tools for comprehensive SEO audits and tracking.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings? +

No ethical SEO professional can guarantee #1 rankings. I guarantee improved visibility, traffic growth, and better search presence through proven strategies.

What's included in your SEO package? +

Technical SEO audit, keyword research, on-page optimization, content strategy, backlink analysis, monthly reporting, and continuous performance monitoring.

Do you work with local businesses? +

Absolutely! I specialize in local SEO: Google My Business optimization, local citations, review management, and location-based keyword targeting.
